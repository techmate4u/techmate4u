## 6.2.3 Content Strategy

- **Blog/News:** Completely absent or not actively indexable. The main site does not have a functional blog subdirectory or subdomain in modern search indexes. They rely purely on static services and "hire developer" landing pages.
- **Case Studies / Work:** Standard "Work" index section demonstrating client variety (e.g., healthcare, real estate, branding, corporate sites).
- **Branding Focus:** Strong historical focus on corporate design, logo design, catalogs, and shade cards. They act as a legacy branding agency that transitioned to full-scale web development.

---

## 6.2.4 Schema & Technical SEO Gaps (TechMate4u Advantage)

1. **Legacy Architecture:** 
   - The site is built on legacy ASP.NET MVC architecture. It uses ASP.NET's traditional bundling mechanism for JS/CSS (`/bundles/main?v=...`), which can lead to slow caching performance and larger unused JS payloads.
   - Heavy synchronously-loading GTM and reCAPTCHA scripts in `<head>`.
2. **Poor Keyword Strategy:**
   - Homepage meta keywords tag is highly stuffed: `"Best Web Development Company, Best Web Design Company..."` (ignored by modern search engines, sometimes flagged as spam).
3. **No FAQPage or Schema Depth:**
   - Only has basic `Organization` schema. Unlike Alakmalak, they lack `FAQPage`, `BreadcrumbList`, or specific `Service` schemas.

**TechMate4u Advantage:** TechMate4u uses Next.js App Router, which yields better Core Web Vitals (FCP, LCP) out-of-the-box compared to legacy ASP.NET MVC configurations. Our technical SEO includes structured JSON-LD schemas (Service, FAQPage, Article, BreadcrumbList) which are missing on Webmantra's static routes.

## 6.2.6 One Clear Differentiator TechMate4u Can Exploit

**Modern Web Tech (Next.js/React) vs. Legacy Enterprise Systems (.NET/PHP)**

- **The Gap:** Webmantra is heavily focused on legacy enterprise software (.NET Core, PHP) and standard CMS/e-commerce platforms (Magento, WordPress). They also have a very corporate, traditional design feel.
- **Our Angle:** TechMate4u is a modern "Digital Product Studio" built on cutting-edge stacks (Next.js, Tailwind, React, Supabase) and AI-first engineering. We can capture clients looking for modern, lightweight, blazing-fast web applications, whereas Webmantra targets traditional enterprises. We should focus our messaging on **"Next-Gen Digital Products"** and **"Lighthouse 99+ Speed"** to contrast our technical agility against their legacy agency size.
