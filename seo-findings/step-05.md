## 5.1 Methodology

Three tiers of competitors were defined based on TechMate4u's target ICPs:
- **Tier 1 — Direct** (same niche, same target client type, directly competing for the same search terms)
- **Tier 2 — Adjacent** (same geography/sector but different positioning; influence SEO landscape)
- **Tier 3 — Watch-list only** (brand confusion risk or future threat; no deep-dive needed)

**Note:** MediaF5 and AONE SEO Service were initially removed after the first research pass because the original attempted URL (`aone-seo.com`) resolved to a UK agency. Both entities were re-verified on 2026-07-15 by fetching their live sites directly. Both confirmed active. See §5.5 for detailed re-verification notes.

## 5.3 Re-verification — MediaF5 & AONE SEO Service

Both were initially listed as "Removed" after the first research pass. Re-verified on 2026-07-15 by direct live site fetches. Both confirmed active. Both reinstated to Tier 2.

### MediaF5 (mediaf5.com)

**Verdict: ACTIVE — Reinstated to Tier 2**

- **Live fetch result:** Site loaded successfully at `mediaf5.com`. WordPress site (Astra theme). Title: `"Best Digital Marketing Company & Agency in Ahmedabad"`. Meta description confirms Ahmedabad positioning.
- **Last modified:** `2026-03-30` per OG `article:modified_time` — actively maintained as recently as 3.5 months ago. Site first published `2025-12-09` — relatively new entrant (under 1 year old).
- **Why excluded originally:** First-pass web search surfaced broad agency lists but didn't return `mediaf5.com` as an explicit result under the name "MediaF5". No direct URL was tested.
- **Services confirmed from homepage:** Digital marketing (SEO, PPC, social media marketing). No web development or automation service visible. Marketing-only agency.
- **Tier rationale:** Tier 2 (adjacent). Competes for "best digital marketing agency Ahmedabad" terms. Does not compete on web engineering or AI automation — low direct threat to TechMate4u's core services, but is present in the same local SERP landscape.

### AONE SEO Service (aoneseoservice.com)

**Verdict: ACTIVE — Reinstated to Tier 2**

- **Root cause of initial exclusion:** A domain lookup error. The query used `aone-seo.com`, which redirected to `a1seo.com` — a completely unrelated agency based in Glasgow, Scotland. The fetch returned an English-language UK SEO site with no Ahmedabad connection. "Could not confirm" was accurate for the wrong URL, but the Ahmedabad entity exists at `aoneseoservice.com`.
- **Correct site confirmed at `aoneseoservice.com`:** Title: `"Best SEO Company in Ahmedabad to Boost Traffic & Sales | AONE SEO Service"`. Geo meta tags: `IN-GJ`. Address meta: `Bopal, Ahmedabad - 380058`. Google site verification token present. Canonical URL set correctly.
- **Entity details:** AONE SEO SERVICE PRIVATE LIMITED (incorporated 2021, operating since ~2013). Google and Meta certified partner. Listed on Clutch, GoodFirms, TechBehemoths, DesignRush.
- **Services confirmed:** SEO (primary service), social media marketing, PPC/Google Ads, ORM, web development (secondary). Industries: real estate, hospitality, food, software. Serves both local and international clients.
- **Tier rationale:** Tier 2 (adjacent). Directly competes for `technical SEO services Ahmedabad` and `best SEO company Ahmedabad` terms. Does not compete on Next.js web engineering or AI automation. Has Clutch presence that TechMate4u currently lacks — worth monitoring their review-building strategy.

## 5.4 Step 6 Deep-dive Schedule

**Scope decision (confirmed 2026-07-15):** Steps 6.1–6.6 cover **Tier-1 competitors only**. This keeps pace with Steps 7–13 without over-indexing on competitor research.

**Tier-2 policy:** Each Tier-2 competitor gets a single-line observation note appended to the relevant Tier-1 deep-dive file where overlap exists (e.g. AONE SEO noted in the Alakmalak file since both compete for the same SEO terms). A Tier-2 competitor is only promoted to a full step if Tier-1 research reveals it is consistently outranking Tier-1 for TechMate4u's target keywords, has a significantly different content strategy worth documenting, or presents a positioning threat not captured by Tier-1 analysis.

Each deep-dive should capture:
- Services offered + primary positioning angle
- Estimated keyword presence (search evidence / SERP snapshots)
- Content strategy: blog, case studies, landing pages
- Schema and technical SEO gaps vs TechMate4u
- Directory/backlink presence
- One clear differentiator TechMate4u can exploit
