- **Services Offered:**

  - CMS development (WordPress, Shopify, custom builds).
  - Data engineering (Databricks, Snowflake).

## 6.5.4 Schema & Technical SEO Gaps (TechMate4u Advantage)

1. **Third-Party Script Weight:**
   - The site loads heavy third-party assets synchronously in the head (Google reCAPTCHA v3, GTM, multiple web fonts).
   - Because they load integrations for Salesforce, Braze, and CDP tags, their mobile Lighthouse performance has script-evaluation delays (our clean codebase is much lighter).
2. **Complex Multi-Domain Redirects:**
   - They redirect traffic between `mavlers.com` (service/positioning site) and `mavlers.agency` (transactional/outsource portal), which can create cookie tracking issues and dilute authority between two distinct domains.

**TechMate4u Advantage:** TechMate4u has a lighter codebase with deferred analytics and zero heavy CRM script overhead, keeping our LCP much lower than Mavlers' heavy enterprise-focused pages. We maintain all authority on a single domain.
