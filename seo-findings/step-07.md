## 7.1 Method

Drawn from our prioritized ICP mappings:
- **ICP 1:** Indian SMB Owner with Manual Workflow Pain (AI & automation systems).
- **ICP 2:** Startup or D2C Brand Needing a High-Performance Website (Next.js, headless commerce, CRO).
- **ICP 3:** International Outsourcing (technical Next.js engineering).

We target 18 high-value parent seed keywords. Crucially, these avoid generic, vanity phrases and focus on realistic intent matching.
